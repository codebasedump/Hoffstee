Paid media refers to any digital marketing effort where businesses pay for ad placements, including search engine ads, social media ads, and display networks.

It provides immediate visibility, drives targeted traffic, and delivers measurable results, making it a key strategy for businesses looking to scale quickly.

Common types include pay-per-click (PPC) ads, display ads, social media ads, native ads, and sponsored content.

  • Paid Media involves purchasing ad space for promotion.
  • Owned Media includes content on platforms you control, like websites and blogs.
  • Earned Media refers to organic publicity, such as media mentions and customer reviews.

Success depends on setting clear goals, understanding the target audience, and selecting the right ad platforms to maximize return on investment.
